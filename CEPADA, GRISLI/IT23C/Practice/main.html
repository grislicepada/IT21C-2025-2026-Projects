PIZZA
Pizza is a popular, savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a leavened bread-based dough, typically a flattened disk, topped with a mixture of tomato sauce, cheese (most commonly mozzarella), and various other ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs, all of which are baked at a high temperature, usually in an oven.
